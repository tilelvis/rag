Bwana asifiwe Turn with me to Matthew 24:9. When we read this passage, we see a God who does not abandon His people. Someone in the back says 'Amen!' and I agree with you, brother. You know, last week I was visiting a family near Fort Ternan, and they told me how the Lord provided during the maize harvest. The maize harvest had made things very hard for them, yet they testified of God's faithfulness. 

"1 Samuel 24:4" let that sink in for a moment. The congregation responds: "Bwana asifiwe!" 

Let me ask you: when was the last time you trusted God in the middle of a storm? Not after the storm. In the storm. There is a difference between praising God on the mountain and trusting Him in the valley. Bwana asifiwe Let us pray. Father, we come before You with hearts that are willing but sometimes weak. Strengthen us. Help us to trust Your Word even when our circumstances shout otherwise. In Jesus' name we pray. Amen.
