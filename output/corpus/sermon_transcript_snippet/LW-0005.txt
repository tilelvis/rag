Good morning, Londiani Worshippers! Before I read the text, let me tell you a story. There was a farmer in Kipkelion West who planted his maize right before the shamba brought challenges. His neighbours laughed. But he said, 'My God will provide.' And He did not the way the farmer expected, but He did. 

Now, Deuteronomy 7:19. This is not just a verse for Sunday. This is a verse for Monday morning when the children need school fees and the shamba has not produced enough. This is a verse for the hospital bedside. This is a verse for the marriage that feels like it is crumbling. 

Paul writes in Philippians 7:21 that nothing can separate us from the love of God. Nothing. Not unemployment. Not sickness. Not even our own doubts. Mungu akubariki, church! Will you believe that today? Shout it out if you believe it! [Congregation: Amen!] Practical application: I want every family here to write down one promise from Scripture and put it on your doorpost this week. Let the Word of God be the first thing you see every morning. Amen.
