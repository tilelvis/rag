Our text today is found in James 14:7. I want to share three truths from this passage. 

First: God sees you. Even in Tulwet, even in your small prayer corner, He sees you. Hagar called Him 'El Roi' the God who sees. 

Second: God hears you. I know some of you have been praying for a long time. Do not give up. 3 John 24:23 reminds us that our prayers are not wasted. 

Third: God will answer. Not always the way we expect. Sometimes the answer is 'yes', sometimes 'wait', sometimes 'I have something better'. But He answers. 

Now I want us to take a moment of silence. [Pause] Whatever you are carrying today lay it at the feet of Jesus. Shall we pray together? Father, You are our refuge and strength, an ever-present help in trouble. We trust You. We lean on You. Bwana asifiwe. In the mighty name of Jesus, amen.
