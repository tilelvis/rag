Turn with me to 1 John 30:22. When we read this passage, we see a God who does not abandon His people. Someone in the back says 'Amen!' and I agree with you, brother. You know, last week I was visiting a family near Kipsitet, and they told me how the Lord provided during the community cleanup. The community cleanup had made things very hard for them, yet they testified of God's faithfulness. 

"James 21:6" let that sink in for a moment. The congregation responds: "Bwana asifiwe!" 

Let me ask you: when was the last time you trusted God in the middle of a storm? Not after the storm. In the storm. There is a difference between praising God on the mountain and trusting Him in the valley. Let us pray. Father, we come before You with hearts that are willing but sometimes weak. Strengthen us. Help us to trust Your Word even when our circumstances shout otherwise. In Jesus' name we pray. Amen.
