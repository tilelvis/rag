MINISTRY UPDATE: DISCIPLESHIP TEAM
Submitted by: Matthias Otieno, Ministry Leader
Reporting Period: Last Quarter

Greetings, Church Board and Pastoral Team,

Here is our quarterly update on the activities and progress of the Discipleship Team.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS:

1. Membership: We currently have 31 active members. This is a steady maintenance from last quarter.

2. Activities Completed:
   • Hosted a fellowship in Nakuru County with 48 attendees
   • Organized three prayer and fasting days
   • Visited 7 home fellowships
   • Conducted 5 training sessions for new members

3. Challenges:
   • Low attendance on weekdays due to work schedules and distance
   • Need for more volunteers in the 3 sub-committees
   • Budget constraints limiting outreach to surrounding areas

4. Upcoming Plans:
   • Retreat planned for next month at a venue near Nakuru County
   • Joint service with neighbouring churches in Kericho County
   • Leadership training workshop

5. Prayer Requests:
   • Wisdom for the leadership team
   • More labourers for the harvest
   • Unity and love among members

Tuko pamoja in Christ.
— Matthias Otieno
