MINISTRY UPDATE: COUPLES' FELLOWSHIP
Submitted by: Matthias Kibiwott, Ministry Leader
Reporting Period: Last Quarter

Greetings, Church Board and Pastoral Team,

Here is our quarterly update on the activities and progress of the Couples' Fellowship.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS:

1. Membership: We currently have 46 active members. This is a 15% increase from last quarter.

2. Activities Completed:
   • Hosted a fellowship in Londiani Catholic Parish with 108 attendees
   • Completed the chama awareness campaign
   • Visited 5 home fellowships
   • Conducted 2 training sessions for new members

3. Challenges:
   • Low attendance on weekdays due to chama
   • Need for more volunteers in the 3 sub-committees
   • Budget constraints limiting outreach to surrounding areas

4. Upcoming Plans:
   • Retreat planned for next month at a venue near Londiani Catholic Parish
   • Joint service with neighbouring churches in Kericho County
   • Leadership training workshop

5. Prayer Requests:
   • Wisdom for the leadership team
   • More labourers for the harvest
   • Unity and love among members

Tuko pamoja in Christ.
— Matthias Kibiwott
