MINISTRY UPDATE: TECHNICAL TEAM
Submitted by: Naomi Kiprop, Ministry Leader
Reporting Period: Last Quarter

Greetings, Church Board and Pastoral Team,

Here is our quarterly update on the activities and progress of the Technical Team.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS:

1. Membership: We currently have 12 active members. This is a 15% increase from last quarter.

2. Activities Completed:
   • Hosted a fellowship in Tulwet with 49 attendees
   • Completed the church construction awareness campaign
   • Visited 8 home fellowships

4. Upcoming Plans:
   • Retreat planned for next month at a venue near Tulwet
   • Joint service with neighbouring churches in Kericho County
   • Leadership training workshop

5. Prayer Requests:
   • Wisdom for the leadership team
   • More labourers for the harvest
   • Unity and love among members
