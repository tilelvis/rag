MINISTRY UPDATE: EVANGELISM TEAM
Submitted by: Stephen Langat, Ministry Leader
Reporting Period: Last Quarter

Greetings, Church Board and Pastoral Team,

Here is our quarterly update on the activities and progress of the Evangelism Team.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS:

1. Membership: We currently have 35 active members. This is a steady maintenance from last quarter.

2. Activities Completed:
   • Hosted a fellowship in Kipkelion West with 114 attendees
   • Completed the baraza awareness campaign
   • Visited 7 home fellowships
   • Conducted 5 training sessions for new members

3. Challenges:
   • Low attendance on weekdays due to baraza
   • Need for more volunteers in the 3 sub-committees
   • Budget constraints limiting outreach to surrounding areas

4. Upcoming Plans:
   • Retreat planned for next month at a venue near Kipkelion West
   • Joint service with neighbouring churches in Kericho County
   • Leadership training workshop

5. Prayer Requests:
   • Wisdom for the leadership team
   • More labourers for the harvest
   • Unity and love among members

Tuko pamoja in Christ.
— Stephen Langat
