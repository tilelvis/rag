Dear K.V.,

Grace and peace to you from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

I am writing to personally welcome you to Londiani Worshippers. Visiting a new church can feel overwhelming, and I want you to know that we are genuinely glad you came. You are not a number to us — you are a person created in the image of God, and we honour your presence.

Our prayer is that you will find Londiani Worshippers to be a place where:

I understand you travelled from Fort Ternan to join us — that speaks of your hunger for God, and He will honour that.

I would love to meet you personally. Please introduce yourself after any Sunday service or schedule a visit through the church office.
