CHURCH BOARD MINUTES
Londiani Worshippers Church Council
=======================================================

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Venue: Conference Room A

ATTENDANCE:
  Present: 11 members
  Absent with apology: 2
  Chair: Stephen Otieno
  Secretary: Chebet Korir

OPENING:
The meeting was opened with prayer by Silas Ochieng. The chair welcomed all members and confirmed quorum.

AGENDA:

1. MINUTES OF PREVIOUS MEETING
   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Chepchumba Njoroge. Second: Samuel Omondi. Carried unanimously.

2. FINANCIAL REPORT
   The treasurer presented the quarterly financial statement. Total income was KES 310,895 against expenditure of KES 232,500. Surplus: Ksh 124,018.
   RESOLUTION: The financial report is hereby adopted. Motion: Deborah Kibiwott. Second: Hannah Kiptanui.

3. CHURCH CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
   The building committee reported that the Sunday School block foundation is complete. Phase 2 (walls and roofing) requires an additional KES 1,193,223.
   RESOLUTION: Approve the release of Ksh 373,132 from the building fund. Motion: Silas Ochieng. Second: Samuel Omondi. Carried.

4. COMMUNITY OUTREACH
   The outreach committee proposed a relief effort for families affected by recent challenges in Molo Road.
   RESOLUTION: Allocate Ksh 51,556 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.

5. PASTORAL MATTERS
   The pastor reported on membership growth and counselling cases (anonymised). Recommendation to hire an assistant pastor was discussed.
   ACTION: The HR committee to prepare a job description and budget proposal for review at the next meeting.

6. AOB
   • The church compound fence needs repair action: Chepchumba Njoroge
   • Christmas programme planning to begin in October
   • Sound system upgrade approved in principle

CLOSING:
The meeting was closed with prayer by the chair at 5:30 PM.
