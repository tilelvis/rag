CHURCH BOARD MINUTES
Londiani Worshippers Church Council
=======================================================

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Venue: Conference Room A

ATTENDANCE:
  Present: 6 members
  Absent with apology: 2
  Chair: Isaac Ruto
  Secretary: Peter Kiprop

OPENING:
The meeting was opened with prayer by Sigilai Githinji. The chair welcomed all members and confirmed quorum.

AGENDA:

1. MINUTES OF PREVIOUS MEETING
   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Jepkosgei Kiptoo. Second: Joseph Sang. Carried unanimously.

2. FINANCIAL REPORT
   The treasurer presented the quarterly financial statement. Total income was KES 545,711 against expenditure of Ksh 432,741. Surplus: Ksh 147,567.
   RESOLUTION: The financial report is hereby adopted. Motion: Kigen Waweru. Second: John Akinyi.

3. CHURCH CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
   The building committee reported that the Sunday School block foundation is complete. Phase 2 (walls and roofing) requires an additional KES 1,180,768.
   RESOLUTION: Approve the release of KES 420,559 from the building fund. Motion: Sigilai Githinji. Second: Joseph Sang. Carried.

4. COMMUNITY CLEANUP RELIEF
   The outreach committee proposed a relief effort for families affected by the community cleanup in Fort Ternan.
   RESOLUTION: Allocate Ksh 22,846 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.

5. PASTORAL MATTERS
   The pastor reported on membership growth and counselling cases (anonymised). Recommendation to hire an assistant pastor was discussed.
   ACTION: The HR committee to prepare a job description and budget proposal for review at the next meeting.

6. AOB
   • The church compound fence needs repair action: Jepkosgei Kiptoo
   • Christmas programme planning to begin in October
   • Sound system upgrade approved in principle

CLOSING:
The meeting was closed with prayer by the chair at 5:30 PM.
