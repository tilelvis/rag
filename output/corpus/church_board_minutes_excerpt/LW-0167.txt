   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Tabitha Wanjiku. Second: Chepchumba Kamau. Carried unanimously.

   RESOLUTION: Allocate Ksh 65,258 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.
