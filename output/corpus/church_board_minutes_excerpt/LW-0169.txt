CHURCH BOARD MINUTES
Londiani Worshippers Church Council
=======================================================

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Venue: Conference Room A

ATTENDANCE:
  Present: 10 members
  Absent with apology: 3
  Chair: Koech Mitei
  Secretary: Rhoda Langat

OPENING:
The meeting was opened with prayer by Moses Kamau. The chair welcomed all members and confirmed quorum.

AGENDA:

1. MINUTES OF PREVIOUS MEETING
   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Thomas Kiprono. Second: Timothy Kipchumba. Carried unanimously.

2. FINANCIAL REPORT
   The treasurer presented the quarterly financial statement. Total income was KES 357,891 against expenditure of KES 438,442. Surplus: Ksh 132,764.
   RESOLUTION: The financial report is hereby adopted. Motion: Lydia Rotich. Second: Lydia Kigen.

3. CHURCH CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
   The building committee reported that the Sunday School block foundation is complete. Phase 2 (walls and roofing) requires an additional KES 1,050,916.
   RESOLUTION: Approve the release of Ksh 296,400 from the building fund. Motion: Moses Kamau. Second: Timothy Kipchumba. Carried.

4. COMMUNITY OUTREACH
   The outreach committee proposed a relief effort for families affected by recent challenges in Koru.
   RESOLUTION: Allocate Ksh 71,528 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.

5. PASTORAL MATTERS
   The pastor reported on membership growth and counselling cases (anonymised). Recommendation to hire an assistant pastor was discussed.
   ACTION: The HR committee to prepare a job description and budget proposal for review at the next meeting.

6. AOB
   • The church compound fence needs repair action: Thomas Kiprono
   • Christmas programme planning to begin in October
   • Sound system upgrade approved in principle

CLOSING:
The meeting was closed with prayer by the chair at 5:30 PM.
