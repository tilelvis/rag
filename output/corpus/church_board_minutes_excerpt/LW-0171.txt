CHURCH BOARD MINUTES
Londiani Worshippers Church Council
=======================================================

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Venue: Conference Room A

ATTENDANCE:
  Present: 9 members
  Absent with apology: 3
  Chair: Samuel Kimani
  Secretary: Joshua Maiyo

OPENING:
The meeting was opened with prayer by Dorcas Githinji. The chair welcomed all members and confirmed quorum.

AGENDA:

1. MINUTES OF PREVIOUS MEETING
   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Koech Ruto. Second: Philip Akinyi. Carried unanimously.

2. FINANCIAL REPORT
   The treasurer presented the quarterly financial statement. Total income was KES 373,914 against expenditure of KES 188,403. Surplus: KES 134,099.
   RESOLUTION: The financial report is hereby adopted. Motion: Cherono Mwangi. Second: Naomi Githinji.

3. CHURCH CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
   The building committee reported that the Sunday School block foundation is complete. Phase 2 (walls and roofing) requires an additional Ksh 573,863.
   RESOLUTION: Approve the release of Ksh 319,351 from the building fund. Motion: Dorcas Githinji. Second: Philip Akinyi. Carried.

4. CATTLE DIP RELIEF
   The outreach committee proposed a relief effort for families affected by the cattle dip in Koru.
   RESOLUTION: Allocate KES 46,714 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.

5. PASTORAL MATTERS
   The pastor reported on membership growth and counselling cases (anonymised). Recommendation to hire an assistant pastor was discussed.
   ACTION: The HR committee to prepare a job description and budget proposal for review at the next meeting.

6. AOB
   • The church compound fence needs repair action: Koech Ruto
   • Christmas programme planning to begin in October
   • Sound system upgrade approved in principle

CLOSING:
The meeting was closed with prayer by the chair at 5:30 PM.
