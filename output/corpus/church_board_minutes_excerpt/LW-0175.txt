CHURCH BOARD MINUTES
Londiani Worshippers Church Council
=======================================================

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Venue: Conference Room A

ATTENDANCE:
  Present: 9 members
  Absent with apology: 1
  Chair: Naomi Koech
  Secretary: Peter Omondi

OPENING:
The meeting was opened with prayer by Esther Kamau. The chair welcomed all members and confirmed quorum.

AGENDA:

1. MINUTES OF PREVIOUS MEETING
   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Chebet Kamau. Second: Dorcas Rotich. Carried unanimously.

2. FINANCIAL REPORT
   The treasurer presented the quarterly financial statement. Total income was Ksh 509,120 against expenditure of KES 302,567. Surplus: Ksh 95,683.
   RESOLUTION: The financial report is hereby adopted. Motion: Rhoda Akinyi. Second: Kigen Mwangi.

3. CHURCH CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
   The building committee reported that the Sunday School block foundation is complete. Phase 2 (walls and roofing) requires an additional KES 916,059.
   RESOLUTION: Approve the release of Ksh 205,553 from the building fund. Motion: Esther Kamau. Second: Dorcas Rotich. Carried.

4. SHAMBA RELIEF
   The outreach committee proposed a relief effort for families affected by the shamba in Tulwet.
   RESOLUTION: Allocate Ksh 75,573 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.

5. PASTORAL MATTERS
   The pastor reported on membership growth and counselling cases (anonymised). Recommendation to hire an assistant pastor was discussed.
   ACTION: The HR committee to prepare a job description and budget proposal for review at the next meeting.

6. AOB
   • The church compound fence needs repair action: Chebet Kamau
   • Christmas programme planning to begin in October
   • Sound system upgrade approved in principle

CLOSING:
The meeting was closed with prayer by the chair at 5:30 PM.
