CHURCH BOARD MINUTES
Londiani Worshippers Church Council
=======================================================

Date: [Meeting Date]
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM
Venue: Conference Room A

ATTENDANCE:
  Present: 11 members
  Absent with apology: 3
  Chair: Kipruto Akinyi
  Secretary: Daniel Ng'eno

OPENING:
The meeting was opened with prayer by Philip Korir. The chair welcomed all members and confirmed quorum.

AGENDA:

1. MINUTES OF PREVIOUS MEETING
   The minutes of the previous meeting were read and adopted. Motion: Dorcas Tum. Second: Solomon Kemboi. Carried unanimously.

2. FINANCIAL REPORT
   The treasurer presented the quarterly financial statement. Total income was Ksh 228,515 against expenditure of KES 437,756. Surplus: KES 102,045.
   RESOLUTION: The financial report is hereby adopted. Motion: Miriam Kiptanui. Second: Thomas Kiprop.

3. CHURCH CONSTRUCTION UPDATE
   The building committee reported that the Sunday School block foundation is complete. Phase 2 (walls and roofing) requires an additional KES 570,634.
   RESOLUTION: Approve the release of Ksh 303,503 from the building fund. Motion: Philip Korir. Second: Solomon Kemboi. Carried.

4. MATATU RELIEF
   The outreach committee proposed a relief effort for families affected by the matatu in Kipkelion.
   RESOLUTION: Allocate Ksh 51,928 for relief supplies. Volunteers to be mobilised through the ministry leaders.

5. PASTORAL MATTERS
   The pastor reported on membership growth and counselling cases (anonymised). Recommendation to hire an assistant pastor was discussed.
   ACTION: The HR committee to prepare a job description and budget proposal for review at the next meeting.

6. AOB
   • The church compound fence needs repair action: Dorcas Tum
   • Christmas programme planning to begin in October
   • Sound system upgrade approved in principle

CLOSING:
The meeting was closed with prayer by the chair at 5:30 PM.
