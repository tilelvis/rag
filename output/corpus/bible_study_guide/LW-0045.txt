📖 BIBLE STUDY GUIDE
Topic: Restoring Broken Relationships
Main Text: Joshua 2:5-10
Memory Verse: Philemon 24:20
Facilitator: Chemutai Waweru
==================================================

OBJECTIVES:
By the end of this study, participants should be able to:
  1. Explain what the Bible teaches about restoration
  2. Identify practical ways to apply the lesson in daily life
  3. Encourage one another through Scripture-based discussion

INTRODUCTION:
As we open God's Word today, let us remember that the Bible is not just an ancient book it is a living word that speaks to our present situation. Many of us in Londiani District Hospital are facing challenges related to the elderly members. But God's truth remains our anchor.

SCRIPTURE READING:
Read Joshua 2:5-10 aloud together. Take turns verse by verse.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS:
  i.   What stands out to you most from this passage?
  ii.  How does this text challenge our understanding of restoration?
  iii. Can you share a personal experience related to this topic?
  iv.  What specific action will you take this week based on this study?

REFLECTION PROMPT:
Spend 5 minutes in silent meditation on Philemon 24:20. Write down one insight the Holy Spirit gives you.

CLOSING PRAYER:
Lord, help us to be doers of Your Word and not hearers only. May the truth we have learned today transform our hearts and actions. In Jesus' name. Amen.
