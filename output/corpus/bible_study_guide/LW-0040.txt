BIBLE STUDY — STANDING ON GOD'S PROMISES

Passage: Deuteronomy 21:10-18
Memory Verse: 1 John 21:14

Welcome, friends! Today we dive into one of the most practical topics in the Christian walk: promises.

STUDY OBJECTIVE:
To understand how God's Word guides us in promises, and to leave this session with at least one concrete step of obedience.

WARM-UP QUESTION:
Think about your life this past week. Where did you see God at work?
(Share in pairs for 3 minutes)

READ & REFLECT:
  • Read Deuteronomy 21:10-18 slowly
  • Underline words or phrases that the Holy Spirit highlights to you
  • Discuss in your small group

KEY QUESTIONS:
  1. What does this passage reveal about God's character?
  2. What does it reveal about human nature?
  3. What command or promise do we find here?
  4. How does this apply to our context in Kericho County?
  5. How does the tea farming season affect our trust in God?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
"1 John 21:14" Can you memorize this by next week?

PRAYER:
Father, open our eyes that we may see wonderful things from Your law. Amen. See you next Wednesday!
