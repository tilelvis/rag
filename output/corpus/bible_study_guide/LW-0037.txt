📖 BIBLE STUDY GUIDE
Topic: The Fruit of the Spirit in Daily Life
Main Text: 2 Thessalonians 7:13-16
Memory Verse: 1 Peter 29:25
Facilitator: Chepchumba Muge
==================================================

OBJECTIVES:
By the end of this study, participants should be able to:
  1. Explain what the Bible teaches about fruit of the Spirit
  2. Identify practical ways to apply the lesson in daily life
  3. Encourage one another through Scripture-based discussion

INTRODUCTION:
As we open God's Word today, let us remember that the Bible is not just an ancient book it is a living word that speaks to our present situation. Each of us carries a burden that only God can lighten. But God's truth remains our anchor.

SCRIPTURE READING:
Read 2 Thessalonians 7:13-16 aloud together. Take turns verse by verse.

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS:
  i.   What stands out to you most from this passage?
  ii.  How does this text challenge our understanding of fruit of the Spirit?
  iii. Can you share a personal experience related to this topic?
  iv.  What specific action will you take this week based on this study?

REFLECTION PROMPT:
Spend 5 minutes in silent meditation on 1 Peter 29:25. Write down one insight the Holy Spirit gives you.

CLOSING PRAYER:
Lord, help us to be doers of Your Word and not hearers only. May the truth we have learned today transform our hearts and actions. In Jesus' name. Amen.
