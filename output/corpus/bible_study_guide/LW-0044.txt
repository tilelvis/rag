BIBLE STUDY — GENEROSITY: LIVING WITH OPEN HANDS

Passage: Jude 39:3-9
Memory Verse: Obadiah 28:12

Welcome, friends! Today we dive into one of the most practical topics in the Christian walk: generosity.

STUDY OBJECTIVE:
To understand how God's Word guides us in generosity, and to leave this session with at least one concrete step of obedience.

WARM-UP QUESTION:
Think about your life this past week. Where did you see God at work?
(Share in pairs for 3 minutes)

READ & REFLECT:
  • Read Jude 39:3-9 slowly
  • Underline words or phrases that the Holy Spirit highlights to you
  • Discuss in your small group

KEY QUESTIONS:
  1. What does this passage reveal about God's character?
  2. What does it reveal about human nature?
  3. What command or promise do we find here?
  4. How does this apply to our context in Koru?
  5. What practical step will you take this week?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
"Obadiah 28:12" Can you memorize this by next week?

PRAYER:
Father, open our eyes that we may see wonderful things from Your law. Amen. See you next Wednesday!
