OUTREACH REPORT — PRISON MINISTRY VISIT

Where: Nakuru County
When: [Date] | Who: Mary Kiptanui + 6 team members
People reached: ~187

WHAT HAPPENED:

We had a powerful time of prison ministry today! The community in Nakuru County was very receptive. Many were struggling because of the rainy season, and they were touched by the church's gesture of love.

HIGHLIGHTS:
  → Shared the Word of God with 187 people
  → Distributed relief items to 28 families
  → Prayed with 12 individuals for various needs
  → 4 people committed to joining a local fellowship

CHALLENGES:
  ✗ Poor road access to some homesteads
  ✗ Insufficient supplies — demand exceeded what we carried
  ✗ Weather was unfavourable due to the rainy season

RECOMMENDATIONS:
  1. Increase budget allocation for community outreach
  2. Visit Nakuru County monthly instead of quarterly
  3. Partner with other churches in the area

Mungu akubariki! The harvest is plentiful.
— Mary Kiptanui
