OUTREACH REPORT — FOOD DISTRIBUTION DRIVE

Where: Londiani Market
When: [Date] | Who: Naomi Kilel + 12 team members
People reached: ~167

WHAT HAPPENED:

We had a powerful time of food distribution today! The community in Londiani Market was very receptive. Many were struggling because of the long rains, and they were touched by the church's gesture of love.

HIGHLIGHTS:
  → Shared the Word of God with 167 people
  → Distributed relief items to 29 families
  → Prayed with 11 individuals for various needs
  → 8 people committed to joining a local fellowship

CHALLENGES:
  ✗ Poor road access to some homesteads
  ✗ Insufficient supplies — demand exceeded what we carried
  ✗ Weather was unfavourable due to the long rains

RECOMMENDATIONS:
  1. Increase budget allocation for community outreach
  2. Visit Londiani Market monthly instead of quarterly
  3. Partner with other churches in the area

Mungu akubariki! The harvest is plentiful.
— Naomi Kilel
