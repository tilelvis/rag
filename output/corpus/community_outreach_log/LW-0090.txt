OUTREACH REPORT — INTERFAITH COMMUNITY DIALOGUE

Where: Kericho County
When: [Date] | Who: Phoebe Mwangi + 4 team members
People reached: ~183

WHAT HAPPENED:

We had a powerful time of interfaith dialogue today! Neema The community in Kericho County was very receptive. Many were struggling because of the harambee, and they were touched by the church's gesture of love.

HIGHLIGHTS:
  → Shared the Word of God with 183 people
  → Distributed relief items to 15 families
  → Prayed with 8 individuals for various needs
  → 6 people committed to joining a local fellowship

CHALLENGES:
  ✗ Poor road access to some homesteads
  ✗ Insufficient supplies — demand exceeded what we carried
  ✗ Short time limited our reach

RECOMMENDATIONS:
  1. Increase budget allocation for community outreach
  2. Visit Kericho County monthly instead of quarterly
  3. Partner with other churches in the area
