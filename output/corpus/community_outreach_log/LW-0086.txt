OUTREACH REPORT — YOUTH EMPOWERMENT WORKSHOP

Where: Londiani Catholic Parish
When: [Date] | Who: Martha Kamau + 11 team members
People reached: ~56

WHAT HAPPENED:

We had a powerful time of youth empowerment today! The community in Londiani Catholic Parish was very receptive. Many were struggling because of the community cleanup, and they were touched by the church's gesture of love.

HIGHLIGHTS:
  → Shared the Word of God with 56 people
  → Distributed relief items to 27 families
  → Prayed with 5 individuals for various needs
  → 7 people committed to joining a local fellowship

CHALLENGES:
  ✗ Poor road access to some homesteads
  ✗ Insufficient supplies — demand exceeded what we carried
  ✗ Short time limited our reach

RECOMMENDATIONS:
  1. Increase budget allocation for community outreach
  2. Visit Londiani Catholic Parish monthly instead of quarterly
  3. Partner with other churches in the area

Mungu akubariki! The harvest is plentiful.
