NARRATIVE:

The team departed from the church compound at 8:30 AM and arrived at Nakuru County at approximately 9:15 AM. We began with a time of prayer and then divided into 4 groups.

Group 1 focused on water project, engaging with approximately 54 Koru.
Group 2 handled distribution of materials and supplies.
