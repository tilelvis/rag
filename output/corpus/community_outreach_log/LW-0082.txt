OUTREACH REPORT — COMMUNITY CLEANUP CAMPAIGN

Where: Londiani Town
When: [Date] | Who: Andrew Ruto + 5 team members
People reached: ~32

WHAT HAPPENED:

We had a powerful time of cleanup today! The community in Londiani Town was very receptive. People opened up about their struggles and we were able to pray with many of them.

HIGHLIGHTS:
  → Shared the Word of God with 32 people
  → Distributed relief items to 15 families
  → Prayed with 11 individuals for various needs
  → 5 people committed to joining a local fellowship

CHALLENGES:

  ✗ Insufficient supplies — demand exceeded what we carried
  ✗ Short time limited our reach

RECOMMENDATIONS:

  3. Partner with other churches in the area
