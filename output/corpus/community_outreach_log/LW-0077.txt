NARRATIVE:

The team departed from the church compound at 8:30 AM and arrived at Kipkelion East at approximately 9:15 AM. We began with a time of prayer and then divided into 2 groups.

Group 1 focused on home fellowship, engaging with approximately 6 neighbourhood families.
Group 2 handled distribution of materials and supplies.
Group 3 conducted a brief worship session and shared the gospel.

KEY OBSERVATIONS:
  • The harambee has significantly affected the livelihood of residents.
  • Many families expressed gratitude for the church's presence
  • 9 people gave their lives to Christ during the outreach
  • Follow-up is needed for 17 individuals who requested prayer
