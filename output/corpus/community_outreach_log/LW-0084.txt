OUTREACH REPORT — MARKET DAY EVANGELISM

Where: Londiani District Hospital
When: [Date] | Who: Eunice Kimani + 10 team members
People reached: ~162

WHAT HAPPENED:

We had a powerful time of evangelism today! The community in Londiani District Hospital was very receptive. Many were struggling because of the dairy farming, and they were touched by the church's gesture of love.

HIGHLIGHTS:
  → Shared the Word of God with 162 people
  → Distributed relief items to 29 families
  → Prayed with 8 individuals for various needs
  → 5 people committed to joining a local fellowship

CHALLENGES:
  ✗ Poor road access to some homesteads
  ✗ Insufficient supplies — demand exceeded what we carried
  ✗ Short time limited our reach

RECOMMENDATIONS:
  1. Increase budget allocation for community outreach
  2. Visit Londiani District Hospital monthly instead of quarterly
  3. Partner with other churches in the area

Mungu akubariki! The harvest is plentiful.
