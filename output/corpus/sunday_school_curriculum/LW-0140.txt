📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 3-5 years (Nursery)
Story: The Good Samaritan — Loving Your Neighbour
Text: Luke 10:25-37

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn that everyone is our neighbour and we should show kindness.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to Luke 10:25-37. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
Luke 10:27 — 'Love your neighbour as yourself.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Neighbourhood map: Draw a map of your neighbourhood and mark ways to help others

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Stephen Maina, Sunday School Teacher
