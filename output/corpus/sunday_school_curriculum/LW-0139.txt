SUNDAY SCHOOL LESSON PLAN
==================================================
Age Group: 12-14 years (Junior Teens)
Lesson Title: The Birth of Jesus — God's Greatest Gift
Scripture: Luke 2:1-20
Teacher: Luke Kigen
==================================================

LESSON OBJECTIVE:
Children will celebrate that Jesus is God's gift to the world.

BIBLE STORY:
Read Luke 2:1-20 from a children's Bible. Tell the story in your own words, using simple language. Ask questions along the way to keep children engaged.

Key points to emphasise:
  1. God is powerful and loving
  2. We can trust God in every situation
  3. God has a plan for each of us

MEMORY VERSE:
Luke 2:11 — 'Today in the town of David a Saviour has been born to you.'
Tip: Say it together three times. Use hand motions if possible.

CRAFT / ACTIVITY:
Nativity scene: Colour and arrange nativity figures on a display board
Materials needed: Scissors, glue, coloured paper, markers
Time: 15-20 minutes

CLOSING PRAYER:
Dear Jesus, thank You for this story from Your Word. Help us to remember what we learned today and to live it out this week. Amen.

TEACHER NOTE:
Relate the story to the children's lives in Nakuru County. For example, when talking about God providing, you might mention how God gives us rain for the maize and grass for the cows.
