📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 3-5 years (Nursery)
Story: Creation — God Made the World
Text: Genesis 1:1-31

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn that God created everything and it was good.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to Genesis 1:1-31. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
Genesis 1:1 — 'In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Creation collage: Draw or paste pictures of things God made on a big poster

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— John Bett, Sunday School Teacher
