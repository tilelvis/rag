SUNDAY SCHOOL LESSON PLAN
==================================================
Age Group: 6-8 years (Lower Primary)
Lesson Title: Peter Walks on Water — Keeping Our Eyes on Jesus
Scripture: Matthew 14:22-33
Teacher: Naomi Rotich
==================================================

LESSON OBJECTIVE:
Children will learn to trust Jesus even when circumstances are frightening.

BIBLE STORY:
Read Matthew 14:22-33 from a children's Bible. Tell the story in your own words, using simple language. Ask questions along the way to keep children engaged.

Key points to emphasise:
  1. God is powerful and loving
  2. We can trust God in every situation
  3. God has a plan for each of us

MEMORY VERSE:
Matthew 14:29 — 'Come, he said. Then Peter got down out of the boat and walked on the water.'
Tip: Say it together three times. Use hand motions if possible.

CRAFT / ACTIVITY:
Water scene: Create a diorama of the storm and Peter walking on water
Materials needed: Scissors, glue, coloured paper, markers
Time: 15-20 minutes

CLOSING PRAYER:
Dear Jesus, thank You for this story from Your Word. Help us to remember what we learned today and to live it out this week. Amen.

TEACHER NOTE:
Relate the story to the children's lives in Kipkelion East. For example, when talking about God providing, you might mention how God gives us rain for the maize and grass for the cows.
