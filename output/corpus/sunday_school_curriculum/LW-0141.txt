SUNDAY SCHOOL LESSON PLAN
==================================================
Age Group: 6-8 years (Lower Primary)
Lesson Title: Jonah and the Big Fish — Running from God
Scripture: Jonah 1-4
Teacher: Miriam Njoroge
==================================================

LESSON OBJECTIVE:
Children will understand that we cannot hide from God and should obey Him.

BIBLE STORY:
Read Jonah 1-4 from a children's Bible. Tell the story in your own words, using simple language. Ask questions along the way to keep children engaged.

Key points to emphasise:
  1. God is powerful and loving
  2. We can trust God in every situation
  3. God has a plan for each of us

MEMORY VERSE:
Jonah 2:2 — 'In my distress I called to the Lord, and He answered me.'
Tip: Say it together three times. Use hand motions if possible.

CRAFT / ACTIVITY:
Big fish puppet: Make a whale puppet from a paper bag and retell the story
Materials needed: Scissors, glue, coloured paper, markers
Time: 15-20 minutes

CLOSING PRAYER:
Dear Jesus, thank You for this story from Your Word. Help us to remember what we learned today and to live it out this week. Amen.

TEACHER NOTE:
Relate the story to the children's lives in Kipkelion. For example, when talking about God providing, you might mention how God gives us rain for the maize and grass for the cows.
