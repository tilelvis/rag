SUNDAY SCHOOL LESSON PLAN
==================================================
Age Group: 12-14 years (Junior Teens)
Lesson Title: Daniel in the Lions' Den — Faithful Prayer
Scripture: Daniel 6:1-23
Teacher: Philip Koskei
==================================================

LESSON OBJECTIVE:
Children will learn the importance of praying faithfully even when it is difficult.

BIBLE STORY:
Read Daniel 6:1-23 from a children's Bible. Tell the story in your own words, using simple language. Ask questions along the way to keep children engaged.

Key points to emphasise:
  1. God is powerful and loving
  2. We can trust God in every situation
  3. God has a plan for each of us

MEMORY VERSE:
Daniel 6:10 — 'Three times a day he got down on his knees and prayed.'
Tip: Say it together three times. Use hand motions if possible.

CRAFT / ACTIVITY:
Lion mask: Create lion masks from paper plates and yarn
Materials needed: Scissors, glue, coloured paper, markers
Time: 15-20 minutes

CLOSING PRAYER:
Dear Jesus, thank You for this story from Your Word. Help us to remember what we learned today and to live it out this week. Amen.

TEACHER NOTE:
Relate the story to the children's lives in Molo Road. For example, when talking about God providing, you might mention how God gives us rain for the maize and grass for the cows.
