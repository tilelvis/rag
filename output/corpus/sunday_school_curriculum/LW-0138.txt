📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 9-11 years (Upper Primary)
Story: David and Goliath — Courage from God
Text: 1 Samuel 17:1-50

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn that with God, even the smallest person can face big challenges.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to 1 Samuel 17:1-50. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
1 Samuel 17:45 — 'You come against me with sword but I come against you in the name of the Lord.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Slingshot craft: Make a simple slingshot from string and cardboard circles

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Elijah Otieno, Sunday School Teacher
