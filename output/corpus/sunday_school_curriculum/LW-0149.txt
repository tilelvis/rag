SUNDAY SCHOOL LESSON PLAN
==================================================
Age Group: 6-8 years (Lower Primary)
Lesson Title: The Prodigal Son — Coming Home to God
Scripture: Luke 15:11-32
Teacher: David Kimutai
==================================================

LESSON OBJECTIVE:
Children will understand that God always welcomes us back with open arms.

BIBLE STORY:
Read Luke 15:11-32 from a children's Bible. Tell the story in your own words, using simple language. Ask questions along the way to keep children engaged.

Key points to emphasise:
  1. God is powerful and loving
  2. We can trust God in every situation
  3. God has a plan for each of us

MEMORY VERSE:
Luke 15:20 — 'While he was still a long way off, his father saw him and was filled with compassion.'
Tip: Say it together three times. Use hand motions if possible.

CRAFT / ACTIVITY:
Welcome sign: Make a 'Welcome Home' banner and role-play the story
Materials needed: Scissors, glue, coloured paper, markers
Time: 15-20 minutes

CLOSING PRAYER:
Dear Jesus, thank You for this story from Your Word. Help us to remember what we learned today and to live it out this week. Amen.

TEACHER NOTE:
Relate the story to the children's lives in Kedowa. For example, when talking about God providing, you might mention how God gives us rain for the maize and grass for the cows.
