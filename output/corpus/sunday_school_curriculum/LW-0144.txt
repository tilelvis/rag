📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 3-5 years (Nursery)
Story: The Lost Sheep — God Never Gives Up on Us
Text: Luke 15:1-7

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will understand that God loves each one of us and searches for us when we wander.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to Luke 15:1-7. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
Luke 15:6 — 'Rejoice with me; I have found my lost sheep.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Cotton ball sheep: Make sheep from cotton balls and construction paper

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Hannah Tum, Sunday School Teacher
