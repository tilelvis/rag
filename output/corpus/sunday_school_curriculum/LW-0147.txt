SUNDAY SCHOOL LESSON PLAN
==================================================
Age Group: 12-14 years (Junior Teens)
Lesson Title: The Wise and Foolish Builders — Building on the Rock
Scripture: Matthew 7:24-27
Teacher: Cherono Mwangi
==================================================

LESSON OBJECTIVE:
Children will understand that obeying Jesus' words is like building a house on solid rock.

BIBLE STORY:
Read Matthew 7:24-27 from a children's Bible. Tell the story in your own words, using simple language. Ask questions along the way to keep children engaged.

Key points to emphasise:
  1. God is powerful and loving
  2. We can trust God in every situation
  3. God has a plan for each of us

MEMORY VERSE:
Matthew 7:24 — 'Everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man.'
Tip: Say it together three times. Use hand motions if possible.

CRAFT / ACTIVITY:
House on rock: Build two houses one on sand, one on a rock and test with water
Materials needed: Scissors, glue, coloured paper, markers
Time: 15-20 minutes

CLOSING PRAYER:
Dear Jesus, thank You for this story from Your Word. Help us to remember what we learned today and to live it out this week. Amen.

TEACHER NOTE:
Use examples the children can relate to from their daily lives. For example, when talking about God providing, you might mention how God gives us rain for the maize and grass for the cows.
