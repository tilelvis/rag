📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 9-11 years (Upper Primary)
Story: Feeding the 5,000 — Sharing What We Have
Text: John 6:1-14

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn that God can multiply our small offerings when we give generously.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to John 6:1-14. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
John 6:11 — 'Jesus then took the loaves, gave thanks, and distributed.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Bread basket: Make a paper basket with five loaves and two fish cutouts

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Naomi Koskei, Sunday School Teacher
