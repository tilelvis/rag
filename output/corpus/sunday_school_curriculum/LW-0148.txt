📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 3-5 years (Nursery)
Story: Joseph's Colourful Coat — God's Plan Even in Hard Times
Text: Genesis 37-50

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn that God has a plan even when things go wrong.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to Genesis 37-50. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
Genesis 50:20 — 'You intended to harm me, but God intended it for good.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Colourful coat: Decorate a coat shape with strips of coloured paper

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Mark Ng'eno, Sunday School Teacher
