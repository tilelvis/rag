📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 9-11 years (Upper Primary)
Story: Moses and the Burning Bush — Answering God's Call
Text: Exodus 3:1-22

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn that God calls ordinary people for extraordinary purposes.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to Exodus 3:1-22. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
Exodus 3:12 — 'I will be with you.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Burning bush: Create a bush from twigs with tissue paper flames

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Hannah Kimani, Sunday School Teacher
