📖 Children's Ministry — Lesson of the Week

Class: 9-11 years (Upper Primary)
Story: Ruth and Naomi — Loyalty and Love
Text: Ruth 1-4

WHAT WE'RE LEARNING:
Children will learn about loyalty, kindness, and how God provides through relationships.

LET'S READ TOGETHER:
Open your Bibles to Ruth 1-4. (For younger children, use the picture Bible.)

TALK ABOUT IT:
  • What happened in this story?
  • How did the people feel?
  • What does this teach us about God?
  • What should we do because of what we learned?

MEMORY VERSE CHALLENGE:
Ruth 1:16 — 'Where you go I will go, and where you stay I will stay.'
Can you say it by yourself next Sunday? 🌟

FUN ACTIVITY:
Wheat craft: Make wheat bundles from straw or paper strips

CLOSING:
Let us pray: Thank You, God, for loving us and for this story. Help us to be brave, kind, and obedient like the people in the Bible. In Jesus' name, amen! 🙏

— Hannah Cheboi, Sunday School Teacher
